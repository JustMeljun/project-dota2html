THIRST

Bloodseeker is invigorated by the wounds of his enemies, restoring some life when he kills a unit. Restores for half values when denying, or if an ally kills a nearby enemy hero. Also gains bonus movement speed when an enemy hero's health falls below 80.0%, with the bonuses increasing as their health falls further.
